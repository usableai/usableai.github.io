### About me

Josie Harrison's research uses machine learning to extract characteristics of the built environment. Before starting her PhD, Josie spent several years working in the architectural, engineering, and construction industry in Canada and the Netherlands. Since graduating from her MSc Data Science, she has focused on finding areas where data science methods can benefit the architectural process.
